About

Made by hand,
head, and machine.

I'm Charlie — a product design student finishing up at University of Oregon, originally from New York City. I design across disciplines: one week it's machined aluminum, the next it's sewing technical outerwear on an industrial machine.

I'm drawn to the tension between craft and industry — things that feel handmade but are rigorously considered. Working toward running my own studio or brand where I can keep making whatever I want.
