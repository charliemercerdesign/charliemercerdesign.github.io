About

I'm Charlie, a product design student at the University of Oregon, originally from New York City. I first became interested in design through building custom mechanical keyboards for clients around the city. What started as a hobby grew into a real fascination with how products are designed, manufactured, and experienced.

Since then I have developed skills across a lot of different areas including woodworking, sewing and pattern making, CAD, and prototyping. I make technical outerwear on industrial sewing machines, laser cut keychains, and design products with real manufacturing considerations in mind. I am drawn to objects that feel handmade but are rigorously thought through.
